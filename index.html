Become a Host Family

Would you like to become a host family for international students from Japan? Access Canada is looking for caring homes in Toronto (GTA), Ottawa, and Montreal. Placements range from 1–4 weeks to several months or a year. If you meet the requirements below, we’d love to hear from you!

Requirements

Provide a safe, comfortable, and English speaking environment for students to thrive.

Private Room

A comfortable private bedroom with a bed, window, desk, closet, chair, and desk lamp.

Meals

Provide 3 nutritious meals a day. Breakfast can be self‑service.

Language

Offer an English speaking environment while the student is present.

Application Process

STEP 1 — Apply

Fill out the online application form. If you have questions, use the contact form — we’re happy to help.

STEP 2 — Confirmation Email

Access Canada will email to confirm your application and next steps. Reply with any details we request.

STEP 3 — Home Visit

We will visit your home to check the living room, dining room, private room, bathroom, laundry, cleanliness, and safety.
